About

My name is Kalun and I was born and raised in Germany. We have a wonderful african grey parrot (Kiwi) and two lovely dogs (Macy and Mimi). I spend most of my free time on my hobbies, which are sports, programming, content creation on YouTube or 3D printing. I love to design and make all sort of things, ranging from nerdy gadges to "improving life" things. The designs are all published on thingiverse.

Career

I studied Business Informatics at the University of Mannheim. During my study, I worked as a teaching assistant, accomplished several internships in the industry and visited many places. After my studies, I worked as an IT-consultant, focusing on optimizing business processes. In 2018, I decided to quit my job and persuade a PhD in the area of computer vision.
