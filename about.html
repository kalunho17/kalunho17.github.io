About

My name is Kalun, born and raised in Germany! I am fluent in 4 languages (Cantonese, Mandarin, German and English) and I occasionally visit my other family members in Hong Kong. Due to Covid-19, I moved back to my parents place near Heidelberg last year. We have a wonderful african grey parrot (Kiwi) and two lovely dogs (Macy and Mimi). I spend most of my free time on my hobbies, which include sports, programming, content creation on YouTube or 3D printing. Feel free to check out my designs or creations here thingiverse or instagram.

Career

During my study, I worked as a teaching assistant, accomplished several internships in the industry and visited many places during my career.
